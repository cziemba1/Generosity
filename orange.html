The Power of Oranges

Oranges are known for their vitamin C content, a powerful antioxidant that helps protect cells from damage. One medium orange will provide the NRV (nutrient reference value) of vitamin C for adults. Oranges are also a good source of fibre, B vitamins, vitamin A, calcium and potassium.
